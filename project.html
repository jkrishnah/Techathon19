Rules

PARTICIPANTS : A Team Of Exactly 3 Members
NO. OF ROUNDS : 2

ROUND 1 : Entry Level Round



  • Participants can choose their own problem statements

  • The overall project idea, abstract and the problem statement is to be presented to the judging team.
  • Selection Parameters: Idea presented, Technological Stack used, Approach in forming the solution.

ROUND 2 : Final Round


  • Project Presentation in front of the judging panel.

  • The judging team will judge the winners based on:
    1. How far the project is developed
    2. The approach they used for solving the problem
    3. The presentation of the working model
