Rules

PARTICIPANTS : A Team Of Exactly 3 Members
NO. OF ROUNDS : 3

ROUND 1 : Entry Level Round



  • MCQ type questions. Questions will be from basics of programing, python, OS, CA, DBMS, Networks, Data Structures, Output Evaluation, Recent trends.

  • Selection will be based on average cut-off. That is in order to qualify to next round you got to make it above the cut off that has been set.

ROUND 2 : Assesment Round


  • Questions from OS, CA, DBMS, Networks, Data Structures, Programming Logic.

  • Questions will be of types MCQ and Fill in the Blanks.

  • Selection criteria will be based on maximum marks scored

ROUND 3 : Final Round


  • There will be four rounds on the final day.

  • Elimination starts from the second round.

  • Two teams on the top of the leaderboard after all 4 rounds, will be the winners and runner ups of the event.
